Alexandra's Sunday Pasta Sauce
------------------------------
Servings: 10

Ingredients:
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 pounds pork spareribs
2 bone-in chicken thighs
2 cups water, divided
2 tablespoons chopped flat-leaf (Italian) parsley
1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
2 pounds pork spareribs
6 cloves garlic

Directions:

7. Add garlic; cook and stir until fragrant, about 1 minute.
8. Pour crushed tomatoes, 1 1/2 cups water, and tomato paste into onion mixture.
10. Pour remaining 1/2 cup water into the roasting pan, and bring to a boil while scraping the browned bits of food off of the bottom of the pan with a wooden spoon.
11. Pour roasting pan water mixture into tomato mixture.
12. Stir Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese, Italian parsley, 2 teaspoons salt, ground black pepper, and red pepper flakes into tomato sauce.
14. Transfer meat to a dish. Adjust sauce seasonings to taste.
